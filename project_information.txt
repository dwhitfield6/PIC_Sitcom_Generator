Hardware Platform: Custom PCB
Debuggers Used: PicKit3
Programmers Used: PicKit3

Description:
This project is used for home automation. The board has an IR receiver, IR LED,
Bluetooth module, a pushbutton switch, 4 dip switches and a 315MHz RF
transmitter. This is used to bridge between bluetooth, IR, and 315MHz RF. Some
RF relays from walmart, homedepot...etc are used at the other end of the
315MHz channel.
